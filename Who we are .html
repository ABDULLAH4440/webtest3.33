Who We Are

Sustainable Builders is a leading force in the mission to create a sustainable and eco-friendly future. We are dedicated to stopping the plastic soup tsunami by addressing plastic pollution at its source. Our single-issue environmental organization focuses on the plastic industry's impact on the environment and human health.

We actively engage with industries and governments, holding them accountable for their environmental responsibilities. Our Plastic Health Summit, campaigns on microplastics, and advocacy for a healthier planet are some of our key initiatives.

Award-Winning Innovations

We take pride in announcing that Sustainable Builders has been honored with the prestigious Prime Minister's Innovations Award 2023. This recognition reflects our commitment to innovative solutions that contribute to a cleaner and sustainable future.

Our Team

Our team consists of dedicated professionals and environmental enthusiasts who share a common goal - building a greener tomorrow. We believe in the power of education and outreach and annually reach over 180,000 children in the Netherlands and Belgium through our free educational programs about plastic pollution.
